How To Train Your Dragon

The animated film titled "How to Train Your Dragon" (original title: "How to Train Your Dragon") was made in 2010, starring Hiccup Hablaty. This movie is about a Viking boy who befriends a special dragon and changes his views about the world and dragons. Hablaty is the character who tries to show his society that dragons are not necessarily enemies, but can be friends and companions. The film received positive reviews for its stunning animation and touching story and was a hit with audiences.
