The Fast and the Furious

These action movies are made for adrenaline and fun seekers. In the series, the characters and the story undergo continuous development, so each new episode holds new adventures and surprises. The first films mainly show the world of illegal car racing, while later the series shifts more and more towards action and chases. The series also has moments that show deeper emotions and relationships between the characters, which further adds to the exciting and entertaining experience of the films. If you haven't seen all the episodes yet, you might want to re-watch the previous ones to recall the story before you start the newer ones. And if you've seen everything, you can wait for the next parts, which will hopefully remain true to the essence and spirit of the series!
