The original trilogy (Episodes IV-VI), produced by George Lucas, was published between 1977 and 1983. These episodes were later followed by further trilogies and supplementary material. The story of the original trilogy takes place in the Galaxy Far, Far Away, where the struggle between the Galactic Empire and the Rebels takes place. The "Star Wars" franchise also includes a lot of secondary material, such as TV movies, animated series, novels, comics and video games. "Star Wars" has not only become an icon of the science fiction genre, but has had a significant impact on pop culture, cinema and the entertainment industry in general. The characters, worlds, and story created a deep and rich universe that captivated generations. "Star Wars" has remained active ever since, with new movies, series and other content to continue to entertain and inspire fans around the world.
