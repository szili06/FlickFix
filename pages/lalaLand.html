La La Land

La la land is a 2016 American musical romantic drama film directed by Damien Chazelle. The film stars Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling, who play the love story of an actress and a jazz pianist. La la land is a modern musical that takes us to the magical world of Los Angeles as two young dreamers try to find a way in the big city to make their dreams come true. Mia, an aspiring actress, and Sebastian, a jazz musician, develop a special bond with each other as they try to find their place in life and art. The film builds heavily on the atmosphere and analyzes of traditional musicals, while at the same time offering a fresh and modern approach to the genre. The stunning music, iconic dance numbers and vivid images of Los Angeles combine to create a stunning cinematic experience that is both romantic and memorable.
