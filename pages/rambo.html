Rambo

"First Blood" introduces audiences to the iconic character of John Rambo, a troubled Vietnam War veteran played by Sylvester Stallone. Set in a small town, the film follows Rambo's tense confrontation with local law enforcement, exploring themes of post-war trauma and the treatment of veterans. Director Ted Kotcheff delivers a suspenseful narrative with explosive action and emotional depth, making "First Blood" a timeless classic in the action genre. If you haven't seen all the Rambo movies, you might want to catch up on them to get a complete picture of the series and enjoy the character's iconic moments and amazing action!
